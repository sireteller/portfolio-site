About Me

Hiya, welcome!

My name is Siret, I am a front-end developer based in Tartu, Estonia. More specifically I am a front-of-the-front-end developer, meaning my expertise is in making web pages beautiful and accessible. CSS is my favourite part of web development, I love watching a page or component come together as I build it.

Front-end development isn't all that's in my heart though, there is plenty of room there for all things creative. On this page I have chosen to showcase my art as well as my coding. I sincerely hope that you enjoy your stay.

PS: If you find any issues or have any suggestions, leave a note on the portfolio site GitHub repository.
